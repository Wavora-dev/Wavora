Wavora is a modern YouTube Music client for Android and Desktop.

Stream music from YouTube Music and YouTube for free, without ads and in the background.

Features:
• High quality audio up to 256kbps
• Synced lyrics with AI translation
• Crossfade with DJ-style transitions
• Spotify Canvas support
• SponsorBlock and Return YouTube Dislike
• Discord Rich Presence
• Offline playback and caching
• Android Auto support
• Sleep Timer
• And much more!
